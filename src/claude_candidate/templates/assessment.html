How This Works

This assessment is powered by claude-candidate, an open-source tool that analyzes real Claude Code session logs alongside your resume to build a grounded evidence profile of your skills. It then maps that profile to the specific requirements of each job posting, scoring three equally-weighted dimensions: skill match, mission alignment, and culture fit. The result is an honest, evidence-backed assessment — not keyword matching — so you know exactly where you stand and what to highlight before you apply.
